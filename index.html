My Journey

I'm a software developer with 1 year and 5 months of experience. I started learning because I was curious about how websites work and wanted to create things that people can actually use. Since then, I've worked on different projects, including an e-commerce and a recipe web application, where I helped build both the front and back ends. Each project has taught me something new whether it's how to connect to a database, make a user-friendly design, or fix errors that took hours to figure out.

I enjoy solving problems, learning by doing, and seeing my ideas come to life on screen. I'm still growing and learning every day, and I'm excited to keep building, improving, and creating better websites as I move forward in this journey.
